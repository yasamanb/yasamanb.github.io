Department of Physics, UC Berkeley


Welcome! I'm a theoretical condensed matter physicist, recently graduated from UC Berkeley.

My graduate training is in the field of quantum many-body physics (strongly correlated electron systems). I have been very fortunate to have Professor Ashvin Vishwanath as my thesis advisor. I've published papers on topological phases, many-body localization, non-Fermi liquids, and topological mechanics. Please see my graduate research. I got started in theory research on quantum systems by working on tensor networks and entanglement as an undergrad with Professor Joel Moore at Berkeley.

I am currently working on research problems in deep learning. Most recently, I was a research intern on the Google Brain team. I would like to help push forward the understanding of neural networks and the design of learning systems.

Please contact me if you have questions, comments, or discussion.
