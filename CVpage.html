Curriculum Vitae

Research Interests

Scientific and theoretical foundations for complex machine learning systems, with a special focus on deep neural networks.

I also pursue multi-disciplinary problems at the interface of machine learning and particular areas of physics, such as dynamical systems and statistical field theory.

Select topics of recent work: optimization and generalization in deep learning; the role of overparameterization; exact connections between neural networks, Gaussian processes, and kernel methods; dynamics under gradient descent; intersection of statistical mechanics and neural networks.
